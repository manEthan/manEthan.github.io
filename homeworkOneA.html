Resources

  • W3schools

    - W3schools is a site that provides several quick and easy-to-follow lessons for if you ever need to learn how to do a specific task, like displaying links with HTML, for example. I find their suggestions to work well most of the time, and I would definitely recommend it as a useful resource.

  • Stackoverflow

    - Stackoverflow is another excellent resource, with a great forum for people to help solve each other's problems. If you have an issue with your code, chances are someone else has experienced a similar difficulty and has had it fixed, and this is one website where you can find those answers.
